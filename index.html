Exhibition Details:

We are excited to announce that the State Level Project Exhibition will take place on April 30, 2024. This event is a significant opportunity for participants to showcase their projects and engage with the broader community of Science & Engineering.

Important Dates and Information:

Date: April 30, 2024

Eligibility: Please ensure your project falls within the eligible domains for the exhibition.

Refreshments: We will be providing light refreshments to keep you energized and engaged throughout the event.

Participation Certificates: Upon successful participation, each participant will receive a hard copy of their participation certificate. These certificates can be included in your final project report, adding a valuable accolade to your academic achievements.

Guide Accompaniment: If your guide is accompanying you, we will provide both participation and attendance certificates, acknowledging the significant role your guide plays in your academic journey.

DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

The Information Science & Engineering department aims to impart the foundational and dedicated skills in design, programming, user interface, etc...

VISION of the Dept

To develop competent professionals with strong fundamentals in Information Science and Engineering, interdisciplinary research and ethical values for the betterment of the society.

MISSION of the dept

M1 - To establish a transformational learning ambience with good infrastructure facilities to impart knowledge and the necessary skill set to produce competent professionals.

M2 - To create a new generation of engineers who excel in their career with leadership/entrepreneur qualities.

M3 - To promote sustained research and innovation with an emphasis on ethical values.
